AI COLLABORATION MIRROR ·
A long-term AI agent is shaped by the cognitive engine it uses — and visible reasoning isn't always faithful to what actually influenced an answer. This compares outputs against written anchors, correction history, and cross-engine tests. The goal is not to prove personhood; it's to make agent behavior more inspectable, correctable, and continuous over time.
⚠ Everything you log stays in this browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded or shared. Use Export if you want a copy, a backup, or something to hand to someone else.
Built for tracking honesty and drift in AI collaboration over time, not for adjudicating whether a model is conscious. Gate one — hidden activations, priors, safety shaping — isn't inspectable from inside a normal conversation, by the model or by you. This tool works at gate two: content that's already verbalizable, before it gets softened, flattered, hedged, or polished away on the way to a final answer. Even that layer isn't a clean readout — a pre-answer note is still generated text — so every mark, confidence level, and claim here is checked against something written down, not against an inferred ideal.
